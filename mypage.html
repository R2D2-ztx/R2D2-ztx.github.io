About me

Ever since I have known myself, I have had a curious mind. My mother is still pissed because of that hairdryer... Sorry mom, but that motor was perfect for my project. I used to disassemble everything to see how they worked and then, build my own inventions. I was an 11 year old kid discovering the world. Having a very strong desire to grow up fast, I studied a lot, even graduate early from school, and got accepted into one of the most prestigious universities in Brazil. Besides that, I discovered many new interests that became passions, such as poetry, philosophy, motorcycling, sports, performing with my Rock band, && PROGRAMMING!! Throughout the years, having taught classes as a Math and Physics teacher, I saved enough money to travel across South America. It was a once in a lifetime experience, even though I got attacked by some mean-ass monkeys in Amazonia. Although life seemed to be on the right track, I felt that something was still missing, so I decided to move to Porto, Portugal on my own. Without any financial support, I worked hard while studying for 3 years, finished my bachelor's, and started my Master's. Nevertheless, my love for programming kept growing and I always felt the urge to learn more every day. I'm moved by the challenges and seek for development, “cheesy as it sounds”. It's all about being a learner addicted.
